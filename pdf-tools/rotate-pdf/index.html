How to rotate PDF

1. Upload your PDF document.
2. Choose the rotation angle (90°, 180°, or 270°).
3. Select if you want to rotate all pages or just specific ones.
4. Click "Rotate & Download" to get your modified file.

Local Processing

Your PDF never leaves your device. All rotation operations happen in your browser's memory using pdf-lib.
