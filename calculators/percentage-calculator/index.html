About Percentage Calculations

This tool covers the three most common percentage use cases: finding a portion, finding a percentage ratio, and finding growth/decline.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find a percentage of a number?

Multiply the number by the percentage and divide by 100.

Can it calculate percentage change?

Yes, it can calculate the percentage increase or decrease between two values.
