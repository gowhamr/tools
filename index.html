Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you start.

Is the tool completely free?

Yes — 100% free, forever. No subscription, no sign-up, no limits. Every feature listed on this page is available at no cost.

Are my files safe and private?

Absolutely. All image and PDF processing runs entirely inside your browser using JavaScript. Your files are never sent to any server — they stay on your device the entire time.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Install the tool via the "Add to Home Screen" prompt (Android/Chrome) or the install icon in your browser's address bar. Once installed, the full tool works without an internet connection.

Which browsers are supported?

Chrome 90+, Edge 90+, Firefox 90+, and Safari 15+. WebP and AVIF export require Chrome/Edge 90+ or Firefox 65+. HEIC reading works in all modern browsers via the heic2any library.

What is the maximum file size I can process?

There is no hard limit imposed by the tool — the practical limit is your device's available RAM. Very large files (over 50 MB) may be slow on lower-end devices.

Will compressed images pass government portal uploads?

The tool implements known portal requirements as of April 2026, but government portals can change their rules at any time. Always verify the current requirements on the official portal before your final submission. Use the Validator tab to check compliance.

Which calculators are included?

16 calculators across four categories: Financial (SIP, Compound Interest, EMI, GST, Discount, Currency), Date & Time (Age, Date, Time, Global Clock), Math (Standard, Percentage, Numeral), and Conversions (Data Storage, Temperature, Speed).
