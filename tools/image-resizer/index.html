How to resize images

1. Upload your image (JPG, PNG, or WebP).
2. Enter your desired width or height.
3. Keep "Lock Aspect Ratio" checked to avoid stretching the image.
4. Choose your export format and quality, then click "Resize & Download".

Privacy Guaranteed

Like all KaruviLab tools, resizing happens directly in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to any server.
