Compound Interest

Calculate future value of a lump-sum investment

About Compound Interest

Compound interest is often called the eighth wonder of the world because interest earns interest. This calculator shows how a lump-sum investment grows when interest is reinvested at a chosen frequency.

Formula used

A = P × (1 + r / n)^(n × t) — where P is principal, r is annual rate, n is compounding frequency, and t is years.
