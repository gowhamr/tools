FAQ & Info

Are my files safe and private?

All image and PDF processing runs entirely inside your browser using JavaScript. Your files are never sent to any server — they stay on your device the entire time.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Install the tool via the "Add to Home Screen" prompt on Android/Chrome or the install icon in your browser's address bar. Once installed, the full tool works without an internet connection.

Is it free to use?

Yes — no payment, no sign-up, no limits. Every feature is available at no cost.

Which browsers are supported?

Chrome 90+, Edge 90+, Firefox 90+, and Safari 15+. WebP and AVIF export require Chrome/Edge 90+ or Firefox 65+. HEIC reading works in all modern browsers via the heic2any library.

What is the maximum file size I can process?

There is no hard limit imposed by the tool — the practical limit is your device's available RAM. Very large files (over 50 MB) may be slow on lower-end devices.

Will compressed images pass government portal uploads?

The tool implements known portal requirements as of April 2026. Government portals can change their rules at any time — always verify on the official portal before submission. Use the Validate tool to check compliance.

Which calculators are included?

16 calculators: Financial (SIP, Compound Interest, EMI, GST, Discount, Currency), Date & Time (Age, Date, Time, World Clock), Math (Standard, Percentage, Numeral), Conversions (Storage, Temperature, Speed).
