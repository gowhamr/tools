Discount Calculator

Find final price and savings after a discount

About the Discount Calculator

Quickly find the final price after a percentage discount and the exact amount you save. Useful during sales, festival offers, or when comparing deals.

Formula used

Final Price = Original Price × (1 − Discount / 100)

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate a discount?

To calculate a discount, multiply the original price by the discount rate and subtract that from the original price.

What is 'Savings' in the calculator?

Savings represents the amount of money you save after the discount is applied.
