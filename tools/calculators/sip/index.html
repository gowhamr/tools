Why use Step-up SIP?

A Step-up SIP allows you to increase your investment amount periodically (usually yearly) as your income grows. Even a small 5-10% annual increase can significantly boost your final corpus due to the power of compounding.

The Real Value (Inflation)

While ₹1 Crore sounds like a lot, inflation reduces its purchasing power over time. If inflation is 6%, ₹1 Crore today will only buy what ₹31 Lakhs buys in 20 years. This calculator helps you see that "Real Value".
