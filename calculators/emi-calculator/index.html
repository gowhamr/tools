Frequently Asked Questions

What is EMI?

Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) is a fixed payment amount made by a borrower to a lender at a specified date each calendar month.

How is loan EMI calculated?

EMI is calculated using the formula: [P x R x (1+R)^N]/[(1+R)^N-1], where P is principal, R is monthly interest rate, and N is number of months.
