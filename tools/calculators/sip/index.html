About the SIP Calculator

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) lets you invest a fixed amount in a mutual fund at regular intervals — typically monthly. Over time, rupee-cost averaging and compounding help grow wealth steadily without timing the market.

Formula used

FV = P × [((1 + r)^n − 1) / r] × (1 + r) — where P is monthly SIP amount, r is monthly rate (annual ÷ 12), and n is total months.
