How to use

Use the buttons or your keyboard: numbers, operators (+, -, *, /), Enter for =, Backspace for CE, and Escape for AC.

Frequently Asked Questions

What operations can this perform?

It performs basic arithmetic like addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

Does it support order of operations?

Yes, most standard calculators follow PEMDAS/BODMAS rules.
