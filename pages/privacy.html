Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

🔒

Short version: We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your files or personal data. All image and PDF processing happens entirely within your browser. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

1. Who We Are

KaruviLab ("the Tool", "we", "us") is operated by R Gowtham under the Wanderseven brand.

  • Owner: R Gowtham

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, process, or store:

  • Any image, PDF, or file you open or process in the Tool
  • Your name, email address, or any other personal information
  • Your IP address or geolocation data
  • Device fingerprints or browser identifiers
  • Usage logs or analytics

The Tool requires no account registration and no login.

3. How the Tool Works (Client-Side Processing)

All file operations — compression, conversion, creation, validation, and PDF processing — are performed entirely within your web browser using JavaScript (the Web APIs provided by your browser). Your files are:

  • Read from your local device via the browser File API
  • Processed in memory using Canvas API, PDF.js, jsPDF, pdf-lib, UTIF.js, and heic2any
  • Saved back to your local device via browser download

No data is transmitted to our servers at any point.

4. Third-Party CDN Libraries

The Tool loads JavaScript libraries from third-party CDN providers (jsDelivr, cdnjs.cloudflare.com, unpkg.com). When your browser requests these scripts, the CDN provider may log your IP address and request metadata as part of standard CDN operation. These logs are subject to each CDN provider's own privacy policy. We do not have access to CDN logs.

Libraries used:

  • jsPDF (cdnjs.cloudflare.com)
  • PDF.js (cdnjs.cloudflare.com)
  • pdf-lib (cdnjs.cloudflare.com)
  • UTIF.js (unpkg.com)
  • heic2any (cdn.jsdelivr.net)
  • Inter font (fonts.googleapis.com / fonts.gstatic.com)

5. Cookies & Local Storage

The Tool does not set any cookies or store any data in your browser's localStorage or sessionStorage. There are no tracking pixels, analytics scripts, or advertising systems on this site.

6. Children's Privacy

The Tool is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through contact with us, please email us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "last updated" date. Continued use of the Tool after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact
