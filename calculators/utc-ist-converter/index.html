About UTC ↔ IST

Indian Standard Time (IST) is UTC+5:30. India does not observe daylight saving time, so the offset is constant year-round.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between UTC and IST?

Indian Standard Time (IST) is 5 hours and 30 minutes ahead of Coordinated Universal Time (UTC).

Does IST have daylight savings?

No, India does not observe Daylight Saving Time (DST).
