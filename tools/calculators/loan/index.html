About the EMI Calculator

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed amount you pay every month toward a loan, covering both principal and interest. This calculator works for home loans, car loans, personal loans, and education loans.

Formula used

EMI = [P × r × (1 + r)^n] / [(1 + r)^n − 1] — where P is principal, r is monthly interest rate, and n is tenure in months.
