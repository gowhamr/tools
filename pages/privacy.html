Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

🔒

Privacy Promise: We believe your data is your own. KaruviLab is designed as a "Serverless Utility," meaning every single operation happens 100% on your device. We do not collect, store, or see your files.

1. 100% Local Processing

KaruviLab is built on the principle of client-side processing. When you use our tools to compress images, merge PDFs, generate QR codes, or format code, the work is done by your own computer or phone's processor using your web browser.

  • No File Uploads: Your files are never sent to a server. They stay in your device's memory until the process is complete.
  • Work Offline: Because the logic is local, most of our tools continue to work even if you disconnect from the internet after loading the page.
  • Private by Design: Since we never receive your data, we cannot leak it, sell it, or lose it.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

To provide maximum privacy, we have eliminated data collection entirely:

  • No Content Logging: We do not log the contents of your text, images, or documents.
  • No Personal Information: We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or address.
  • No Accounts: There are no user accounts, passwords, or profiles to manage.
  • No Tracking: We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixels, or any other third-party tracking scripts.

3. How We Use Browser Technology

We use modern browser APIs to make this privacy possible:

  • File API: To read files from your device locally.
  • Canvas API: To process and compress images in your browser's memory.
  • Web Workers: To handle heavy processing without slowing down your interface.
  • Service Workers (PWA): To allow the application to load and work without an internet connection.

4. Third-Party Libraries & CDNs

While the processing is local, the Tool loads some open-source libraries to function. These are served via global Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) to ensure fast loading speeds. When your browser fetches these libraries, the CDN providers (like Cloudflare or jsDelivr) may see your IP address as part of the standard web request process. We choose reputable, privacy-conscious providers.

Note: We have bundled the QR Code library locally to ensure it works 100% offline without any external requests.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

KaruviLab does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. We use the browser's localStorage only to remember your preferred visual theme (Light or Dark mode). This data never leaves your device.

6. Security

Since no data is transmitted to our servers, the primary security of your files depends on the security of your own device and browser. We recommend using an up-to-date browser for the best security and performance.
