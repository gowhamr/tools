About the Age Calculator

Calculate exact age in years, months, and days from any date of birth. Also shows total days lived, total months, and total weeks.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the age calculator work?

It calculates the difference between your date of birth and the current date or a specific date in years, months, and days.

Is it accurate for leap years?

Yes, the calculator accounts for leap years and varying month lengths.
