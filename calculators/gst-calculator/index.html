GST Calculator

Add or remove GST from a base price

About GST

Goods and Services Tax (GST) is India's unified indirect tax system. This calculator lets you add GST to a base price or extract the base price from a GST-inclusive amount.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is GST?

Goods and Services Tax (GST) is an indirect tax used in India on the supply of goods and services.

How is GST calculated?

GST can be calculated by adding the GST percentage to the base price or by extracting it from the total price.
